Edge Island

The only way the 5,500 artists of Edge Island can defend their island home from vicious attack is to find the Eyeris and draw from its infinite creative power. Some artists use their powers to control and destroy, while others use their powers to defend everything and everyone they love. A war is coming and whatever side your artist is on, one thing is certain, you won't survive unless you level up...

A.O.T.E. (Artist Of The Eyeris) is a collection of 5,500 NFT's on the Ethereum platform. These NFTs are based on the comic/manga book series characters written and illustrated by artist and A.O.T.E. founder Cory Evans. As an NFT holder, you join an exclusive artist collective that has one simple aim: To bring notoriety and profitability to artists while expanding the A.O.T.E IP and Community.

Book Series

Artist of the Eyeris Is a manga/comic book series written and illustrated by artist (and A.O.T.E. project founder) Cory Evans. Each book is 150+ pages of a fantasy/action/adventure story that follows artists, endowed with supernatural abilities, as they fight impossible odds to save what matters to them most. LEVEL UP YOUR ARTIST NFT!

A.O.T.E. allows you as an NFT holder to LEVEL UP your artist, should you so choose, from Level 0 all the way to Level 10. When you choose to LEVEL UP your NFT, your artist is endowed with greater powers and the newly created NFT gives you new art and even more perks. The level indicator is in the upper left corner of every NFT and will change as you level up.

Every A.O.T.E. NFT starts at Level O. Ninety days after each drop of original art NFT's (according to the drop schedule), you will have free access to your next level NFT through the minting website.
As an AOTE NFT holder, you can choose to LEVEL UP your artist by burning your current level NFT to receive the next level NFT, which is created by a different professional real world artist! The burning of the current level NFT is meant to ensure scarcity and high value for all art collectors at each level.

HOW TO LEVEL UP YOUR ARTIST NFT

At any time that you join the A.O.T.E. as an NFT holder and collector (no matter your level), you'll receive the appropriate number of level upgrades from your current level, so you can power up your artist and keep up with all the other artists out there fighting the war on Edge Island.

FOUNDER'S PROFILE

Cory Evans

Born in New York City, Cory Evans (aka SEE-EVANS) has been a professional contemporary artist for over a decade. Creating countless drawings, canvas paintings, and murals for exclusive clientele and public schools in New York, New Jersey, Philadelphia, and others including Miami where he currently resides. An award-winning artist, Evans one-of-a-kind artistic style has garnered him numerous accolades, including a proclamation by the Mayor of Miami.

Expanding his creations to digital art, he started drawing and writing AOTE manga series in 2015 and now the first volume is finally ready to share with the world. Having big goals for the brand, the team has created an NFT project that allows people to take this artistic journey with us. As an artist, he wrote this story to speak to the artist in us all.
